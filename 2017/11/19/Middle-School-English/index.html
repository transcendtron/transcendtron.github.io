必修一

Unit1 Friendship

1. upset
  • Tony didn’t pass the exam, which made him very upset.
  • The bad news that the plane was missing upset him.
  • be upset at/about/over sth./doing sth.为……心烦
2. ignore故意不理睬 ignorance ignorant无知的 be ignorant 不知道
  • I said hello to Susan this morning, but she ignored me.
  • be in ignorance of/about sb./sth.
  • be ignorant of
  • Last week a tennis ball hit me on the head，but I tried to ignore the pain, believing that it would go away sooner or later.
3. calm人的情绪或者无风 quiet 环境或者内心 silent 人不发出声音，无声的状态 still 静止不动 完全没有声音 静止的状态
  • You should keep calm in face of danger.
  • calm down, keep/remain/stay/ calm
4. concern担心be concerned about/for,concern oneself with sth,concern oneself about/for sth.,as/so far as…be concerned就…而言，show/express concern for/ahout
  • There is a concern about the effects of cellphone radiation手机辐射的影响concerned,concerning
5. series a series of一连串的
  • Anne wrote down her deepset feelings besides a series of facts in her diary.series 单复同形的名词。besides 及其
